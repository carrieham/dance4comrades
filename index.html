A solidarity fundraiser for striking workers!


University and College Union (UCU) members at 145 universities across the UK have been engaged in a nation-wide marking boycott since April 2023.The boycott comes after a breakdown in negotiations with the employers' association (UCEA) over rampant casualization, overwork, soaring student-staff ratios, and low pay in UK higher education.Following threats and actions by Senior Management at Queen Mary University of London (QMUCU) to deduct pay from workers who have ceased marking, members of the Queen Mary UCU (QMUCU) local branch have engaged in additional local strikes.
